Naiqi Li received his Ph.D. in Data Science and Information Technology from Tsinghua University in 2022. Currently, he is a postdoctoral researcher at the Tsinghua Shenzhen International Graduate School.

Naiqi Li's research areas include multimodal perception and generation, knowledge representation and reasoning, probabilistic machine learning, and signal analysis. He has published over 20 papers in top AI conferences and journals, such as NeurIPS, AAAI, IJCAI, UAI, ICASSP, ECAI, and IEEE TAI.
